# AC-007

## Criterion

The leaderboard ranks players by score descending, total time ascending, and join time ascending as the final tiebreaker.

## Linked Requirements

- FR-QUIZ-015 (leaderboard ranking)
- BR-006 (quiz ranking)

## Verification Steps

1. Create a live quiz with at least three players.
2. Player A finishes with 8/10 score and 120 seconds total time.
3. Player B finishes with 8/10 score and 90 seconds total time.
4. Player C finishes with 9/10 score and 200 seconds total time.
5. Open the leaderboard view as a student or admin.
6. Compare displayed order.
7. In standard mode, confirm any in-progress player shows `?` for score and is sorted as if score = 0.

## Expected Result

Final order: C (9/10), B (8/10, 90s), A (8/10, 120s). When two players tie on score and time, the player who joined first (lower `joined_at`) ranks higher. The race view animates rank deltas with horse positions matching the score progress.
