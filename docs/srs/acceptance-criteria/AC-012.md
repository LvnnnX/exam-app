# AC-012

## Criterion

Hidden and admin-only categories do not appear in student-facing category selection.

## Linked Requirements

- FR-EXAM-005 (apply visibility settings)
- BR-002 (hidden question exclusion)
- BR-003 (student category visibility)

## Verification Steps

1. Sign in as admin. Open Settings.
2. Mark MAPEL `MTK-OPT` as `Hidden`.
3. Mark BAB `Aljabar-Linear` as `Admin only`.
4. Save.
5. Open `/` as a student in a private window.
6. Open the MAPEL dropdown. Confirm `MTK-OPT` is absent.
7. Pick another visible MAPEL that contains `Aljabar-Linear`.
8. Open the BAB dropdown. Confirm `Aljabar-Linear` is absent.
9. Inspect the network response from the categories endpoint. Confirm the hidden / admin-only rows are filtered server-side, not just client-side.

## Expected Result

Hidden categories never reach the student bundle. Admin-only categories are excluded from student-facing fetches. The student dropdowns reflect the saved visibility settings on the next page load.
