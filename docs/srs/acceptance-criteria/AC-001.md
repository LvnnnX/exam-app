# AC-001

## Criterion

A student can complete a strict self-paced exam end-to-end and view both score and recap.

## Linked Requirements

- FR-EXAM-001 (mode picker)
- FR-EXAM-002 (navigation picker)
- FR-EXAM-003 (name input)
- FR-EXAM-004 (categories)
- FR-EXAM-006 (time limit)
- FR-EXAM-007 (question count)
- FR-EXAM-008 (confirm identity)
- FR-EXAM-009 (start session)
- FR-EXAM-014 (strict navigation)
- FR-EXAM-021 (submit)
- FR-EXAM-022 (score view)
- FR-EXAM-023 (recap view)

## Verification Steps

1. Open `/`.
2. Step 1: type a name (≥ 1 char), pick `Exam`, then `Strict`, pick MAPEL, BAB, Sub-bab (each multi-select), pick a time limit, pick a question count.
3. Tap `Lanjutkan`. Step 2 (`Confirm identity`) appears with the chosen values.
4. Tap `Start exam`. Runtime opens on question 1.
5. Confirm the header has `Soal No. 1`, mode chip `Exam`, topic chips with `+N` pattern, timer pill, and saved/pending chip.
6. Confirm the action bar shows `Next question` and `Skip` (no Back, no daftar-soal).
7. Answer every question by tapping option pills and `Next question`.
8. On the final question, the button label flips to `Finish`. Confirm the submit modal copy.
9. Confirm submit. Score view appears.
10. Tap `Lihat recap`. Recap view appears with per-question rows.

## Expected Result

The exam completes without navigation errors. The score view shows correct/total, percentage with tone-aware tint (green/blue/orange/red), topic chips, save status chip, and `Lihat recap` + `Ulangi` pills. The recap view lists every question with the student's answer (green/red/neutral) and the correct answer.

## Out-of-Scope For This Criterion

- Survival behavior (covered in AC-003).
- Free navigation behavior (covered in AC-002).
- Live quiz flows (AC-004 through AC-007).
